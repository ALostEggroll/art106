Brush-bot

This is my spider-like brush bot. This was just my second time soldering something together and it proved to be a difficult project. One problem that constantly occured was the bot breaking due to not being soldered properly. Another problem was that my wires were too long and it would often cause my bot to fall down. But with trial and error I corrected my mistakes to the best of my abilities and created this. Overall it was a fun assignment as it felt like this was the very first "robot" I have ever made.
