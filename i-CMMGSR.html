Choose My Music GSR

Proposal / Artist Statement

I often find it difficult to decide which music I want to listen to based on the different activities I do. So as a solution I decided to use my GSR to decide what type of music to listen to based on how the music would make me feel. For instance, if I was stressing out on homework, I would probably want to listen to something that would calm me and help me finish an assignment. On the otherhand, if I was working out and I couldn't get my last rep in because of fatigue, I would want to listen to something that would excite me to help push through my workout. My GSR helps me decide what to listen to based on how a particular song would excite or calm me.

I listened to a couple of songs and recorded my excitement and calmness levels using the GSR to catagorize if the music made me feel more excited or more calm. I improved the software display to have a simple line that acted as the center threshold where my average excitement/calmness level would often reside. If the GSR read more above the line, the song would be catagorized as an exciting song. If lower than the line, the song would be a calm song. I then used this to create a playlist of exciting and calming songs to help me pick songs based on how they made me feel.
