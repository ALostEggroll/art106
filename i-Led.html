Wearable LED


My wearable LED focused on that habit where people who wear glasses would often adjust it in many different ways. Mine focused on the middle press motion, where the LED's would light up if one pushed their finger onto the middle section of their glasses. This was a fun project where I got to feel like Tony Stark creating an accesory that lights up based on a subcontious habit.
